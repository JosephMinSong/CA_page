Diet Guidelines for Immunosuppressed Patients

You may have decreased immune function because of chemotherapy and/or radiation therpay or from taking medciations to suppress your immune system. This means that you are at increased risk of developing a food-related infection. The purpose of this diet is to help you avoid specific foods that are more likely to contain infection-causing organisms while allowing maximum healthy food choices. Choose foods from the "May eat" column and don't choose foods in the "Do Not Eat" column. You may want to discuss the sfaety of these or other foods with your dietitian or care team.
